Delivery and Returns

1. ORDERING & PAYMENT

ALL PRODUCTS ON YOUNG-GRAFTERS.COM ARE IN STOCK AND AVAILABLE FOR ORDER. ITEMS IN YOUR SHOPPING CART ARE NOT RESERVED. PROCESSED ORDERS ARE SUBJECT TO PAYMENT ACCEPTANCE AND AVAILABILITY. YOUNG-GRAFTERS.COM ACCEPTS PAYPAL. WHEN USING PAYPAL, PLEASE ENSURE YOUR SHIPPING ADDRESS IS CORRECT.

2. SHIPPING

ALL PACKAGES ARE PROCESSED FOR SHIPPING WITHIN 24-48 HOURS OF YOUR PURCHASE. ESTIMATED DELIVERY IS TO BE USED AS A GUIDE ONLY. DELAYS ARE NOT THE RESPONSIBILITY OF YOUNG-GRAFTERS.COM WE DO NOT SHIP TO P.O. BOXES.

3. INTERNATIONAL ORDERS

WE ACCEPT INTERNATIONAL ORDERS. INTERNATIONAL CREDIT CARDS WILL BE CHARGED IN ACCORDANCE WITH APPLICABLE EXCHANGE RATE ON THE DAY YOUR CARD ISSUER PROCESSES THE TRANSACTION.

DUE TO THE LIMITED AMOUNT OF GARMENTS WE HOLD, CANCELLATIONS AND RETURNS MAY BE REFUSED. WE RESERVE THE RIGHT TO TURN DOWN CANCELLATIONS AND RETURNS.

5. EXCHANGE

ALL EXCHANGES MUST BE AUTHORIZED BY EMAIL, info@young-grafters.com ITEMS MUST BE RECEIVED WITHIN 7 DAYS OF RECEIPT OR UNLESS OTHERWISE NOTED. ALL PRODUCTS RETURNED MUST BE IN ORIGINAL UNUSED CONDITION, WITHOUT ANY TAGS REMOVED. PRODUCTS RETURNED DAMAGED, WORN OR ALTERED MAY NOT BE ACCEPTED AND MAY BE SENT BACK TO CUSTOMER. ALL CHARGES RELATED TO SHIPPING ARE YOUR RESPONSIBILITY AND ARE NON-REFUNDABLE. WE RESERVE THE RIGHT TO TURN DOWN EXCHANGES.
